Buffer and BulkPublish

BulkPublish is not owned or operated by Buffer. BulkPublish is a companion tool for professional publishers who use Buffer. BulkPublish leverages Buffer's infrastructure for scheduling your posts. Therefore, all limits set by Buffer's plans apply to your BulkPublish accounts. If you are hitting limits on your Buffer, consider checking out their Awesome Plan or their Business Plans.
